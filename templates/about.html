About NIT-Campus mart

A student-built marketplace for student needs — because we know the struggle of buying new every semester.

🎯

Our Mission

To create a trusted, verified marketplace where college students can buy and sell used academic items, reducing waste and saving money for every student.

🛡️

Why Trust Us?

Every user is verified through their college Google account. Every listing is reviewed by our admin team before it goes live, ensuring quality and authenticity.

💸

Zero Platform Fees

NIT-Campus mart is completely free to use. No commissions, no listing fees, no hidden charges. What you see is what you pay.

🌱

Sustainability

By giving used items a second life, we help reduce unnecessary waste on campus and encourage a culture of sharing and sustainability.
